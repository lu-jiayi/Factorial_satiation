Instructions

In this experiment you will read English sentences, and determine if they sound acceptable to you. By acceptable, we mean whether you think a native speaker of English could say this sentence in a conversation. In other words, do you think it would sound odd for your friends to say this to you, as if they don't speak English natively? We are not concerned with whether the sentence would be graded highly by a writing teacher.



Instructions

You will rate the sentence on a scale from 1 (unacceptable) to 7 (acceptable) by using your mouse or the number keys on your keyboard. Here are three examples: a very bad one with a rating of 1, one that is in middle of the scale, and a very good one with a rating of 7.


The was insulted waitress frequently.          Rating: 1


Tanya danced with as handsome a boy as her father.          Rating: 4


This is a pen.          Rating: 7
